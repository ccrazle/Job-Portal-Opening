# Rejection

**When to use:** When a candidate is not selected after evaluation.

---

## Email Format

**Subject:** Update on Your Application — {Job Title} at {Company Name}

---

Dear {Candidate Name},

We regret to inform you that you were not selected for the applied role **{Job Title}** at **{Company Name}**. We appreciate the time and effort you put into the interview process.

We encourage you to apply for future opportunities that align with your skills and experience.

Thank you once again for your interest in our company.

Best regards,
{HR Name}
{Designation}
{Company Name}

---
