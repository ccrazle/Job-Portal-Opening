# Selection / Offer

**When to use:** When a candidate is selected and you are extending the offer.

---

## Email Format

**Subject:** Congratulations! You've been selected — {Job Title} at {Company Name}

---

Hello {Candidate Name},

Congratulations! We are pleased to inform you that you have been selected for the **{Job Title}** role at **{Company Name}**.

**Offer Details:**
- **Position:** {Job Title}
- **Department:** {Department}
- **CTC:** {CTC Offered}
- **Joining Date:** Please confirm your date of joining
- **Office Timings:** {Office Timings}
- **Reporting To:** {Manager Name, Designation}
- **Work Location:** {Location}

We look forward to having you on our team.

A formal offer letter with complete terms and conditions will be shared with you shortly. Please confirm your acceptance by replying to this message.

Best regards,
{HR Name}
{Designation}
{Company Name}

---

## Placeholders

| Placeholder | Description | Example |
