# Interview Invite

**When to use:** When scheduling an interview with a shortlisted candidate.

---

## Email Format

**Subject:** Interview Invitation — {Job Title} at {Company Name}

---

Dear {Candidate Name},

You have applied for **{Job Title}** post at **{Company Name}**. We want to invite you for the interview at our {Office Location}.

**Interview Details:**
- **Location:** {Company Name}, {Full Address} ({Google Maps Link})
- **Date:** {Date}
- **Time:** {Time}
- **Contact Person:** {HR Name} ({HR Phone})

Please bring a copy of your updated CV.
*Before visiting for interview please confirm your total experience with relevant domain experience and current salary.*

Kindly confirm your availability by replying to this message.

Best Regards,
{HR Name}
{Designation}
{Company Name}
