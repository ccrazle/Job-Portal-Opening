What Is the Job Portal?

The Job Portal brings every step of opening a new position — writing the description, picking job boards, screening applicants, and reaching out to top candidates — into one place. No more switching between email, Excel trackers, and multiple job board tabs.

Built specifically for One Group's HR team, it comes pre-loaded with your company's departments, roles, and job descriptions so you can post a new opening in under two minutes.

Creating a Job Opening

Opening a new position is designed to be as fast as possible. Instead of filling out a long form from scratch, you pick the role from One Group's internal database and everything fills in automatically — job description, skills, location, screening questions, everything.

Step 1 — Pick the Role

Select a department, then choose a job title. The moment you pick a title, 12 fields fill in automatically from the company database: location, employment type, level, experience range, salary, reporting line, required skills, preferred skills, screening questions, and the full job description.

How Auto-Fill Works
Pick Department → Choose Title → Everything fills in automatically

Step 2 — Review & Approve

One scrollable page where you can check and edit everything before posting. It has four sections:

A

Job Description

Already filled in from the database. You can edit it directly, or upload your own Word document (.docx) to replace it entirely. A small label tells you where the current text came from.

B

Choose Job Boards

The system recommends the best platforms for this type of role (e.g., LinkedIn for leadership, Apna for entry-level). You can override any recommendation. Below the selection, a "Portal-Ready Format" section shows you exactly what to paste into each job board's form, with one-click copy buttons.

C

Screening Questions

Pre-loaded questions from the database, ready to use. You can add your own custom questions (text, multiple-choice, or yes/no) and reorder them. A warning appears if you exceed 5 questions, since some job boards have limits.

D

Final Review

A summary card showing everything you're about to post — job details, description excerpt, skill tags, and selected platforms. One last check before it goes live.

At the bottom you'll find Save as Draft (come back to it later), Approve & Post (make it live), or — when editing an existing posting — Move to Draft and Delete options.

Outreach Management

Once you've screened candidates and marked the best ones as "Confirmed," the Outreach section helps you manage the next step — actually contacting them.

First — Pick an Opening

You'll see a grid of all your posted openings that have confirmed candidates. Each card shows the job title, location, and how many candidates are ready for outreach. Openings with no confirmed candidates don't appear here, so you're only looking at roles that need action.

Then — Manage Candidates

Click an opening to see its confirmed candidates in a detailed table. From here you can track the outreach status of each person:

StatusWhat It Means
PendingYou haven't reached out yet
On HoldThe candidate is thinking it over
ConfirmedThey've accepted and are moving forward
Not InterestedThey've declined the opportunity

You can filter by status, tier, or search by name. A "Back to Openings" button at the top takes you back to the opening list at any time.

Your Data

Everything you do in the portal — drafts, posted openings, candidate scores, outreach statuses — is saved automatically in your browser. There's no login, no cloud sync, and no server to worry about. Your data stays on your computer.

What Gets Saved

DataWhen It Saves
Draft OpeningsEvery time you click "Save as Draft" or move a posting back to draft
Posted OpeningsWhen you approve a new opening or save changes to an existing one
Screening ResultsWhen you import resumes, change a candidate's status, or mark someone as a referral
Outreach ProgressEvery time you update a candidate's outreach status

Job ID Format

Every opening gets a unique ID that looks like OG-2026-001, OG-2026-002, etc. The number increases automatically as you create new openings. This ID appears on posted cards and in the review summary so you can always reference a specific opening.

Important: Since data is stored in your browser, clearing your browser data will also remove your portal data. Don't clear browsing data for this site unless you've exported what you need.

In Summary

The Job Portal replaces the scattered, manual process of managing job openings with a single tool that handles everything from start to finish. No more switching between spreadsheets, email threads, and multiple job board tabs.

The system is built around one principle: speed without sacrificing control. Auto-filled forms save time, but every field stays editable. AI-recommended platforms reduce guesswork, but you can always override. Automatic tier assignment handles the sorting, but you choose who gets contacted.

With 61 roles across 13 departments pre-loaded, 7 job boards ready to use, and a screening-to-outreach pipeline that tracks every candidate from import to confirmation — the portal is ready for your team to use today.
